Our Story

A little corner of Paris in Toronto.

Café de Flore Patisserie was born from a simple belief: that life is richer with beautiful food and unhurried moments. Every croissant we fold, every sandwich we assemble, every cup we pour — it's an act of care for the people who walk through our door.

We're proud to be expanding across Toronto, sharing the kind of experience that turns a coffee break into a memory.
